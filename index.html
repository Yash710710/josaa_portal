  • Seat Matrix 2023

    • The seat matrix of Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) for the year 2023 is represented in the pie chart below. The chart illustrates the distribution of seats among the different IITs based on their respective seat capacities. The pie chart provides a visual representation of the number of seats available in each IIT. Each slice of the pie corresponds to a specific IIT, and the size of the slice indicates the proportionate number of seats allocated to that particular institution. The chart reveals the variations in seat capacities across the IITs. Some IITs have a larger number of seats, while others have a relatively smaller number. This distribution reflects the different admission capacities and focus areas of the individual IITs. Please refer to the chart to get an overview of the seat matrix of IITs in the given year.

  • NIRF Ranking 2023

The NIRF rankings are based on various parameters such as teaching, learning, research, perception, and outreach.
